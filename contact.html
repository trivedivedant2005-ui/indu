Frequently Asked Questions

What are the admission requirements?

+

Candidates should have a Bachelor's degree in any field recognized by HNGU. Merit-based and entrance exam-based admissions are available. Visit our admissions page for detailed criteria.

What is the fee structure for MSc CA & IT?

+

The fee structure is available on our website and portal. We also offer several scholarship schemes based on merit and financial need. Contact the finance office for detailed information.

How can I reset my portal password?

+

Click on "Forgot Password" on the login page and enter your enrollment ID. A reset link will be sent to your registered email. Follow the instructions to set a new password.

What is the placement scenario for graduates?

+

Our institute maintains a 95% placement rate with avg CTC 6.5 LPA. Students are placed in leading IT companies like TCS, Infosys, Cognizant, Wipro, and others.

Are hostel facilities available?

+

Yes, we have separate boys' and girls' hostels with modern amenities. Limited seats are available and allocated based on merit and distance from campus. Contact the hostel office for details.

Can I contact faculty directly?

+

Yes, faculty members have designated office hours and can be contacted through the student portal. You can also reach them via email at [faculty email addresses listed on portal].
